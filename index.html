Unity Game

You stranded on an seemingly uninhabited island. You need to hunt for food and gather resources to survive. You can chop down trees to get wood and gather stones, from these materials you can make a house and weapons. While exploring the island, you meet monsters and bosses which you can fight to get special items.

Arduino project

In this project I made a phone safe, you can put your phone in it when you need to focus on work. It opens up after half an hour, that way you won't be distracted when you need to finish an asignment, or if you can't resist to grab your phone when you need to sleep.

First I designed the box in fusiom 360 (as shown in the picture). After that I used a laser cutter to make a prototype. That is where the project is now but I hope to continue and finish it to make a working product.
